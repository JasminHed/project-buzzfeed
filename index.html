Cozy Coding Cafés in Stockholm

Where Wi-Fi is strong, coffee is flowing, and you actually get work done!

Tired of working from home? Need a change of scenery but still want to stay productive? Stockholm is packed with amazing cafés that aren’t just good for fika but also perfect for coding, studying, or remote work. Whether you’re looking for cozy corners, big tables for team projects, or just a quiet place with solid Wi-Fi, we’ve got you covered.

Here’s our ultimate list of the best coding cafés in Stockholm—tried, tested, and approved by laptop warriors like you. 

Konditori Lyran

Work with a view—just like Selma Lagerlöf once did

Perched on a hill in Bredäng with a stunning view over Lake Mälaren, Konditori Lyran isn’t just a café—it’s a piece of Swedish literary history. In the late 1800s, the villa was a cultural hotspot where authors, musicians, and artists gathered for lively soirées hosted by Fredrika and Carl Limnell. Selma Lagerlöf, Henrik Ibsen, and even King Oscar II were among the guests, and it’s said that the Swedish expression “att vara på lyran” (meaning to be tipsy) originates from these legendary gatherings.

Aside from its rich history, Konditori Lyran is a great workspace. With spacious indoor seating, peaceful surroundings, and a breathtaking outdoor terrace, it’s an inspiring spot to focus or collaborate. Their classic Swedish fika, featuring homemade cakes and pastries, makes for an excellent work break. Beware, though—weekends can get quite crowded, and the peaceful atmosphere quickly turns into a lively buzz..

More Info:

  • Address: Lyranvägen 9
  • Closest subway: Bredäng (then a 10 min walk)
  • Opening hours: Mon–Sun 11am–5pm
  • Price range: $$
  • Work-friendly features: Scenic view, quiet atmosphere (except on weekends!), inspiring historic setting.

Il Caffé

Best workplace

Il Caffè is more than just a café—it's a space designed for productivity. With its inviting atmosphere, excellent coffee, and quiet rooms, it's the perfect place to focus and get work done.

More Info:

  • Address: Södermannagatan 23, 116 40 Stockholm
  • Closest subway: Medborgarplatsen or Skanstull
  • Opening hours: Mon-Sun 8am-5pm
  • Price range: $$
  • Work-friendly features: spacious, small breakout rooms, teamwork.

Drop Coffee

Excellent coffee

Located at Mariatorget in Stockholm, Drop Coffee offers a warm and inviting atmosphere where coffee enthusiasts can enjoy expertly brewed, speciality coffee.

More Info:

  • Address: Wollmar Yxkullsgatan 10, 118 50 Stockholm
  • Closest subway: Mariatorget
  • Opening hours: Mon-Fri 8am-6pm, Sat-Sun 10am-5pm
  • Price range: $$
  • Work-friendly features: Intimate, smaller breakout rooms, calm during weekdays.
